Classic Tetris

Experience the timeless puzzle game that has captivated players for decades. Arrange falling tetrominoes to create complete horizontal lines and watch them disappear. The game speeds up as you progress through levels, challenging your reflexes and strategic thinking.

Features:

  • Classic Tetris gameplay with 7 unique tetromino shapes
  • Progressive difficulty with increasing speed
  • Score system with combo multipliers
  • Level progression every 10 lines cleared
  • High score persistence using localStorage
  • Pause/resume functionality
  • Smooth animations and modern UI
  • Hard drop for quick placement

Scoring System:

  • Single Line: 100 × Level
  • Double Lines: 300 × Level
  • Triple Lines: 500 × Level
  • Tetris (4 Lines): 800 × Level
  • Hard Drop: +2 points per row

Built With:

Angular TypeScript HTML5 Canvas CSS3 Game Dev

Pro Tips:

  • Always leave space for the I-piece (the long one)
  • Try to maintain a flat surface for easier stacking
  • Use hard drop (SPACE) to quickly place pieces
  • Plan ahead - think about where the next piece will fit
  • Clear multiple lines at once for higher scores
  • Don't panic when the speed increases - stay focused!
