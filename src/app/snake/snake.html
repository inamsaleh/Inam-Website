Classic Nokia Snake

Relive the nostalgia of the classic Snake game that made Nokia phones legendary in the late 90s and early 2000s. This recreation captures the authentic feel of the original game with its iconic green screen and pixel-perfect gameplay.

Features:

  • Authentic Nokia-style green monochrome display
  • Classic snake movement and controls
  • Score tracking with high score persistence
  • Responsive keyboard controls
  • Retro pixel-perfect graphics
  • Progressive difficulty as snake grows

Built With:

Angular TypeScript HTML5 Canvas CSS3

How to Play:

  • Arrow Keys: Control snake direction (Up, Down, Left, Right)
  • Goal: Eat the food (blinking square) to grow longer
  • Avoid: Running into walls or your own body
  • Score: Each food item is worth 10 points
  • Restart: Press SPACE after game over
